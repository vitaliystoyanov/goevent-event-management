Goevent is a smart tool for successful event organizers.

Features

EVENT PAGE

Choose a cover photo, add description, additional text, contacts, tickets and location. Compose and publish a professional inviting page in less than 5 minutes.

TICKETS

Attendees receive an email confirming their tickets. Add your custom tickets to event page.

CHECK IN ATTENDEES

Search guest names and check them in on your phone or tablet with Goevent Organizer app

ORGANIZER'S PANEL

Get a snapshot of your events' attendees or tickets got.

SCAN TICKETS

Use the in-built camera to scan tickets as they arrive. Both mobile and paper tickets are supported.

MULTI-USER ACCESS

Grant your team access to your events.
